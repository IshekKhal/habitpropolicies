Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 27, 2025

1. Introduction

HabitPro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application (the "App") collects, uses, and discloses your information.

2. Data We Collect

  • Account Information: We collect your name and email address when you sign in via Google or Apple Sign-In.

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous crash reports and performance metrics.

3. AI & Third Parties

We use trusted third-party services:

  • Google Gemini AI: Processes chat messages to provide coaching.
  • Supabase: Securely stores user database records.
  • RevenueCat: Manages store subscriptions.

4. How We Use Data

To provide coaching, manage subscriptions, and send notifications.
